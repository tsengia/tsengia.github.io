About

8 Bit Bio is a site for interactive lessons, diagrams, and games for biology. It targets an
audience of teens and young adults to give them an interesting way of learning. Other sites and
resources aim to do the same, though they are outdated, slow, boring, and overall not appealing to
modern society. We hope to teach students solid facts and have them be able to memorize them for a
lifetime's worth of use. The site also aims to utilize methods to appeal to all the different learning styles
to maximize the understanding of the students.

We hope you enjoy and learn from this site.

~ExpandingDev and Braytos Daskhan
