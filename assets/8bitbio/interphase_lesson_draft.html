Interphase

  In the cell cycle there are multiple stages. The first and longest stage is interphase.
Interphase is the time when a cell is growing and preparing for cell division.


1st stage of the cell cycle
Longest stage of the cell cycle
  • Duplicates DNA
  • Produces more organelles
  • Growth + Preparation
    • for cell division

There are several sub-stages of Interphase.

  • G1
  • 1st sub-stage of Interphase
  • Grows in size

  • synthesizes mRNA and proteins



  • The cell can go into 2 sub-stages after G1, either
  •               G0 or S
  • G0
  • Sort of a hibernation stage
  • S Phase
  • DNA Duplicated


  •     The final sub-stage of interphase is     
                        G2
  • Produces Proteins needed for Mitosis
  • Checks for DNA damage
